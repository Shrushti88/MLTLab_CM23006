What is MobileNet?
MobileNet is a pre-trained CNN trained on ImageNet — 1.2 million images across 1,000 object classes. It already knows how to classify dogs, cats, vehicles, food, furniture, and hundreds more. We load its pre-trained weights and call classify() — no training needed. Everything runs in your browser.
