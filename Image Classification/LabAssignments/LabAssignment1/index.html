How MobileNet classifies your image:
1. Your image is resized to 224×224 pixels internally.
2. Pixel values are normalised to [−1, 1].
3. A forward pass runs through 53 convolutional layers.
4. A softmax output gives a probability for each of 1,000 ImageNet classes.
The top-3 highest probabilities are displayed as predictions.
